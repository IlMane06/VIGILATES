CORPORATE SOFTWARE

"Intelligent Surveillance Plus" software is the result of the company's arduous research and advanced development in the field of security and of technology. Designed to revolutionize surveillance, this cutting-edge software synergistically connects street cameras and not produced by us or any of its affiliated companies, integrating artificial intelligence to provide superior security and understanding detail of the surrounding environment.

Main features:

  • Advanced Object Recognition:
    The software uses powerful artificial intelligence algorithms to accurately identify a wide range of objects and situations, including vehicles, pedestrians, abandoned objects and more. This feature allows you to detect and automatically report potentially dangerous situations, greatly improving the ability to prevent accidents.

  • Color Recognition and License Plate Reading:
    Thanks to the integration of AI, the software is able to recognize the colors of objects and, in particular, to automatically read vehicle license plates. This feature not only facilitates traffic monitoring, but it is also crucial in scenarios of searching for specific vehicles or individuals.

  • Real Time Analysis:
    The software analyzes video streams from cameras in real time, allowing surveillance operators to react promptly to unforeseen or suspicious situations. Alerts are generated automatically, providing crucial information for decisions fast and effective.

  • Intuitive Dashboard:
    The user interface is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring that even novice traders can take advantage of take advantage of the advanced features of the software. The clear and detailed visualizations allow constant and complete monitoring.

  • Adaptability and Scalability:
    The software is highly customizable to fit the specific surveillance needs of different areas or situations. It is also designed to be easily scalable, allowing for the addition of new cameras and features as needs grow.

Benefits:

  • Enhanced Security:
    Advanced object recognition and real-time situation analysis allow you to locate potential threats quickly and react in a timely manner.

  • Operational Efficiency:
    Automating video analytics reduces the workload of human operators, allowing them to focus on tasks of greater importance.

  • Accident Prevention:
    The ability to detect dangerous situations helps prevent traffic accidents and other emergencies.

  • Better Search and Recovery:
    Automatic license plate reading and color recognition make search and recovery easier of vehicles or individuals involved in illegal activities.




HOW IS IT DEVELOPED BY EMPLOYEES?

( part performed by me as an intern )

Day after day, the constant commitment of our employees translates into an activity that goes beyond the development of software improvements, clearly visible in our periodic updates. They devote a considerable amount of time and resources to education and refinement of our internal artificial intelligence network. This process involves a meticulous selection operation by the employees, who carefully examine thousands of images for characteristics such as color, conformity and content. This rich pool of images comes next transmitted to the neural network, providing a substrate of data from which to draw in order to learn and improve. It is through this collaboration between human attention to detail and the power of artificial intelligence that our neural network is constantly learning and honing its ability to discern, gradually approaching the error threshold towards an increasingly insignificant deviation from the point of perfection, i.e. zero.
